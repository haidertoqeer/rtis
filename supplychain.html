What is IM-Stock

IM-Stock is a real-time electronic Logistics Management Information Systems (eLMIS) that automates the logistics function and processes of inventory and warehouse management of the public health logistics across all tiers of supply chain of a country. The IM-Stock aggregates, analyze, validate and display data reported into system from all levels; that can be used to manage supply chain and helps to take operational and policy decisions.IM-Stock addressed following which were difficult to accomplish in manual system.

What is Implementation

IM-Stock is a real-time electronic Logistics Management Information Systems (eLMIS) that automates the logistics function and processes of inventory and warehouse management of the public health logistics across all tiers of supply chain of a country. The IM-Stock aggregates, analyze, validate and display data reported into system from all levels; that can be used to manage supply chain and helps to take operational and policy. decisions.IM-Stock addressed following which were difficult to accomplish in manual system.

1. Improve recordkeeping: complete, updated stock and consumption records.

2. Improved reporting compliance – enabled health manager to monitor reporting and non-reporting health facilities on its dashboard.

3. Data usability – data is visible and available by single click for decision making.

The Features That We Are Providing

In this modern era, integration of the system is essential to have one repository to triangulate data from prevailing information systems. Therefore, interoperability is fundamental requirement of any system to ensure effective utilization of data for decision making, and to provide maximum value and flexibility to countries and programs.

Through Application Programming Interface (API) based architecture of the IM-Stock enables other systems to communicate to each other for two way data movement.
